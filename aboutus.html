Who We Are

Welcome to Farming Tales, where we bring together farmers, agri-enthusiasts, experts, and concerned citizens to cultivate a sustainable future for agriculture in the Philippines. Founded in 2023, our community aims to be a haven of resources, advice, and support for anyone interested in farming and rural development across the archipelago.

Cultivating A Sustainable Future for Philippine Agriculture

Our Mission

We are driven by the mission to uplift the agriculture sector in the Philippines by fostering innovation, knowledge-sharing, and community engagement. We believe that by working together, we can tackle the challenges that our farmers face and create a robust, sustainable agricultural framework for the nation.

Our Vision

We envision a Philippines where farming is not only profitable but also sustainable and beneficial for local communities. We aim for a future where farmers can earn a fair living, young people see agriculture as a promising career path, and where sustainable farming practices are the norm, not the exception.

What We Do

Educational Content: From the basics of planting rice to modern hydroponic systems, our blog posts, webinars, and how-to guides offer a wealth of information.
Community Building: Through our online forums and local meet-ups, we provide a space for members to share, learn, and collaborate on farming ventures.

Advocacy: We lobby for fair trade, better resources for farmers, and for sustainable agriculture policies at both local and national levels.
Resource Sharing: Members gain exclusive access to farming tools, seeds, and even financial grants aimed at encouraging sustainable agricultural practices.
